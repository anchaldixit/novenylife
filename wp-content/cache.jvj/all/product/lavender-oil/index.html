Benefits Of Noveny Lavender Oil

Acne Control: Lavender oil works to kill bacteria, prevent and heal acne breakouts.

How To Use: Add 2-3 drops of lavender oil in 10 ml of jojoba oil and apply as a moisturizer on a clean face to reduce pimples and acne scars.

Hair care: Lavender oil helps in promoting hair growth and fights dandruff.

How to use: Mix 2-3 drops of lavender oil with 10 ml of coconut oil and massage hair and scalp to reduce dandruff.

Skin Lightening: Lavender oil can aid in skin lightening since it reduces inflammation.

How to use: Mix 2-3 drops of Lavender oil with 10 ml of moisturizer for wrinkles and skin massage.

Body Massage: Lavender oil is the perfect addition to massage oil to de-stress and relieve tired, tense muscles.

How to use: Blend 2-3 drops of Lavender oil with a carrier oil of your choice for a relaxing massage.

Refreshing Aroma: Lavender oil used in diffusers as its smell so good to reduce stress & anxiety.

How to use: Use 2-3 drops of lavender oil in diffuser to make the surrounding aromatic with soothing Lavender aroma.

NOTE: Always use diluted essential oils before use as they are highly concentrated and should be handled with caution.Refer Noveny’s Essential oil dilution guide or consult our experts.

Easy Ways To Use Lavender Oil

Relaxing sleep: Add 2 drops of lavender oil on your pillow for a restful sleep.

Mix with carrier oils: Mix few drops of Lavender Oil with carrier oil to use on skin, hair and face.

Removes bad odour: Use 2-3 drops of Lavender Oil regularly in water while taking bath.
